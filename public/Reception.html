Rawatbhata, Rajasthan

Reception/ Homecoming of Bride to the Groom’s residence will take place in Rawatbhata, Rajasthan. Rawatbhata is a small town in south eastern Rajasthan, about 630 kms from Delhi and is 50 km from the nearest city Kota. It is a calm and serene place, with natural beauty all around. It is the place where Groom spent his growing up years, and the reception would be a get together of family & friends over dinner.
